About Miza Establishment

A dedicated facility management and support services partner, focused on excellence, quality, and long‑term client relationships across diverse industries.

Introduction & History

Miza Establishment specializes in delivering comprehensive facility management and support services. Founded with a commitment to quality, the company has grown steadily by consistently meeting client needs and building a trusted reputation across multiple sectors.

Our experienced team brings years of expertise in facility management, enabling us to design and operate solutions that enhance safety, efficiency, and overall performance of client properties.

Vision, Mission & Core Values

Our vision is to become the most reliable partner in facility services, exceeding customer expectations through innovation, professionalism, and sustainable practices. Our mission focuses on delivering measurable value and superior service quality in every engagement.

Integrity, accountability, customer focus, teamwork, and continuous improvement form the foundation of our culture, ensuring every project is executed with the highest ethical and operational standards.
